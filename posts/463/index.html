Legends

January 27, 2021

The cloud fell deeply in love with the sky from the day it saw it.

Cloud said that the sky was a myth in her heart and she could not resist the power of God.

So she said to the sky, “I love you! Love your brilliant blue!”

The sky smiles, “I belong to all things and cannot be attributed to your love.”

The sea is also deeply in love with clouds from the day it sees them.

Because the love in his heart is as white as the clouds.

But he loved in his heart because he knew that Cloud had fallen in love with Sky.

Perhaps God destined him to be a latecomer.

The clouds are listed in the love of the sky

The sky is always smiling at her from afar.

Not too far and not too close.

Is the breadth of the sky unable to accommodate an admiring love?

Cloud cries secretly.

Bitter love, bitter love ……

The sea is aching in the heart.

What is intolerable is a crush crying.

The sea vows, “I will want to be like the blue sky and then nestle my clouds!”

So the sea is changing itself.

He shall be wide, spreading over seven tenths of the surface of the earth.

He wants azure, accepting the show of rivers and lakes ……

Even so, the sea can not change the gap between him and the sky, the

The sea is always so small ……

Heaven is still looking up at the world in the realm of nothingness.

Condescendingly indifferent to the presence of clouds.

The clouds cry secretly at the edge of love and pain.

The white clouds have become ominous clouds.

The rain of sadness is dripping, it is bitter tears.

Her tears fell into the heart of the sea and

Stirring up the angry waves of the sea.

He pounded hard on the scorched stones of the shore.

Then the breath of buried love is knocked into broken froth.

The cloud could not stop the flow of tears for three days and nights,

The sea also used his heart for three days and nights,

The sea has become salty

Those are the tears of the clouds filling the sea of suffering

The clouds know that, from a distance

There is a sea watching her,

But she didn’t look back at the person watching her.

A heart can only hold one myth.

Finally, one day, the

The sea says to the clouds, “I love you! I always look up to you with love!”

Cloud’s melancholy eyes shone with pain and

She will not turn her back on her faith in love.

And then to chase another unthought of love!

The clouds did not respond to the sound of the sea

In love and unlove.

She chose to run away.

Thus.

Right in the middle of the sky and the sea.

Clouds learn to drift.

The sky does not belong to the clouds, and the clouds do not belong to the sea!

This is the end:

The clouds did not throw themselves into the arms of the sky,

The sea is not touched by the clouds either.

From then on, the sea looked up to the clouds, and the clouds looked up to the sky.

The cloud floats between two hearts,

I can no longer find the sky I flew in

and the sea that can be used as a dressing mirror ……
